| **EEG-Recon** | eeg_reconciliation | 3 | ✅ | **SIGNIFICANCE REPRODUCED, MAGNITUDE NOT — the appendix's ≈12 is optimistic.** Isolated the cause of the 1.7-vs-12 gap on the same 15 cached subjects. Switching *only* that estimator lifts the median ratio 1.28 → 3.33 (14/15 > 1) and makes it significant in 14/15 (median p≈0.0025, near the appendix's single-subject 5×10⁻⁴; Wilcoxon vs unity p≈6×10⁻⁵) — so the appendix's **direction and significance replicate**. The qualitative claim (real, significant, replicated structural discrimination) stands; the specific magnitude figure should be corrected downward. |
